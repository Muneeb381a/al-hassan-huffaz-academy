Our Story

From a Humble Beginning to a Landmark Institution

AlHassan Huffaz Academy was founded in 2009 by a group of passionate educators and community leaders who believed that Islamic education and academic excellence should never be separate.

What began as a small Hifz circle in a single room has grown into one of Sialkot's most trusted Islamic educational institutions — serving hundreds of families across Rangpura and surrounding areas.

Over 15 years, we have graduated more than 500 Huffaz, consistently achieved strong board results, and built a faculty of dedicated, qualified teachers who consider teaching a form of ibadah.

2009 Academy founded with 12 students and 2 Qaris in Rangpura, Sialkot
2012 Academic wing opened — Classes 6 & 7 added to the curriculum
2015 First batch of 20 Huffaz graduated. New campus building inaugurated
2018 Girls section fully established with dedicated Qarias and female staff
2022 500th Hafiz graduated — a milestone celebrated by the entire community
Today Serving 300+ active students across Hifz and Academic programs
